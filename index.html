Privacy Policy for Love Messages

Last Updated: October 26, 2025

JoshTechApps ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle and protect your information when you use our mobile application, Love Messages (the "App"). The App provides a platform for users to browse, create, and share romantic messages, quotes, and images.

1. Data Processing Overview

The following describes how data is processed when you use the App:

a. User-Generated Content

  • Custom Messages & Quotes: The App allows you to create and save custom love messages and quotes. These messages are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers unless you choose to share them.
  • Feedback: If you submit feedback through the App, we may receive your email address and feedback content when you choose to submit via email.
  • Photos and Media: If you use the camera feature or upload images for custom backgrounds, these images are stored locally on your device and are not sent to our servers unless you share them.

b. Third-Party Data Processing

  • Analytics Services: Our app uses Firebase Analytics which may collect anonymized data about how you interact with the App. This includes information such as which features you use, session duration, and navigation patterns. This data does not identify you personally and is used to improve the App's performance.
  • Crash Reporting Services: Our app uses Firebase Crashlytics which may collect anonymized data about app crashes and performance issues. This may include device information and crash logs to help diagnose and fix issues.
  • Advertising Services: The App uses Google AdMob, Unity Ads, and Vungle to display ads. These advertising services independently collect anonymized data, such as device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID), IP address, and general location data, to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.

c. App Permissions

The App requests the following permissions for specific features:

  • Camera: Used to take photos for custom quote backgrounds (optional).
  • Storage: Used to save or share images of quotes you create.
  • Media: Used to access images from your gallery for custom backgrounds.
  • Internet: Required for displaying ads, sending feedback, and analytics services.
  • Notifications: Used to send daily love quote notifications (optional).

d. Notification Data

If you enable notifications, we use Android WorkManager to schedule and deliver love quote notifications. These notifications are generated from local content and do not require server interaction. Your notification preferences are stored locally.

2. How Data Is Used

Data is used for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Improve the App: Usage data and crash reports help understand how the App is used and identify areas for improvement.
  • To Personalize Your Experience: User-generated content and settings are used to customize your experience within the App.
  • To Deliver Notifications: If enabled, we use your selected notification categories to send relevant love quotes.
  • To Display Ads: Advertising partners use anonymized data to serve relevant ads.
  • To Respond to Feedback: If you submit feedback, we use your email address and feedback content to respond and provide support.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, data may be processed by third-party services as follows:

  • Third-Party Services:
    • Google Firebase: Usage data and crash reports are processed by Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics.
    • Google AdMob: Anonymized data is processed by AdMob to deliver ads.
    • Unity Ads: Anonymized data may be processed by Unity Ads for advertising purposes.
    • Vungle: Anonymized data may be processed by Vungle for advertising purposes.
  • User-Initiated Sharing: If you share quotes or images via email, social media, or other apps, the shared content is transmitted through the selected third-party app.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: User-generated content and settings are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers unless you share it.
  • Third-Party Storage: Analytics and crash data are processed by Firebase and stored on Google's servers. Ad-related data is processed by advertising partners.
  • Security Measures: We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, though no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

5. Your Choices

  • Permissions: You can deny or revoke permissions in your device settings.
  • Notifications: You can enable or disable notifications in the App's settings.
  • Advertising: You can opt out of personalized ads by adjusting your device's Advertising ID settings.
  • Data Deletion: Since user-generated content is stored locally, you can delete it by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may process data:

  • Google's Privacy Policy (for Firebase and AdMob)
  • Unity Ads Privacy Policy
  • Vungle Privacy Policy

8. International Users

The App is hosted and operated in the United States. If you use the App from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
